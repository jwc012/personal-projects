General FAQ

What's included in my package?
  • All packages come with: slimes/items ordered, borax, a care sheet, and random fun extras.
Do you use borax in the slimes?
  • Yes, we use a diluted borax solution.

SHIPPING + HANDLING

Where is my order?
  • Check the tracking number provided either in your email or on your order confirmation page. Please be patient as sometimes it may take the post office time to scan/update your package info. If your tracking number has not been updated for a while, please contact us for further assistance.

    • → 3-5 days processing time + 2-7 days transit

  • Processing time: the period of time between the date an order is placed and when it is shipped Transit time: the period of time it takes to receive an item after it has been sent out for delivery Note: during the holidays (ie: Christmas) the processing/transit time may take longer.
What do I do if I receive my package damaged?
  • Please contact us immediately with your order number and a picture of the damage item/package.
What do I do if I got the wrong slime?
  • Please contact us immediately with your order number and a picture of the incorrect item you received.
What do I do if my package was returned to sender (Fancy Squeeze)?
  • Your package was most likely sent back due to incomplete address. Once we receive the package you will be refunded for the slimes however, shipping cost will not be refunded.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

As of right now, we have not opened our shop to international shipping. Once we do, we shall announce it on our website/ Instagram @fancysqueeze!

REFUNDS + CANCELLATIONS

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Cancellations are not accepted after the order has been placed. If you are underage and using a parent’s credit card, please get parental permission before purchasing! Refunds are reserved for mistakes made on our end. Ex. wrong slime received, incomplete order, etc.
