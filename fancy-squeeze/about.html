About Me

Fancy Squeeze was started March 2017 by a curious girl in her Southern California home. After coming across a variety of slime videos on Instagram Fancy become intrigued by all the unique textures and qualities slime possessed. She first started experimenting with recipes she found online, and then started creating her own unique textures/scent blends with materials from all over the world! As her love for slime grew she decided to share her creations with others via her online shop and as of recently in person slime events. Fancy draws inspiration for her creations from simple everyday objects such as grocery market items, or even nature itself. Each creation is handmade with love and thoughtfully crafted!
